ENICMA is a GitHub organisation that aims to develop software tools, algorithms, AI programs, and various engineering techniques for the life sciences. The primary objective of ENICMA is to enhance bioinformatics tools by refining existing algorithms and developing new ones to effectively address biological challenges. Additionally, ENICMA aims to convert the innovative methods and techniques derived from the field of bioinformatics into video documentation, allowing for their widespread dissemination on the internet.

ENICMA's overarching objective is to secure financial backing and establish a team of volunteers dedicated to advancing diverse engineering methods within other biotechnology domains. The aim is to foster the creation of numerous open-source projects, thus cultivating an ecosystem where biotechnologists can freely exchange ideas and collaborate on joint initiatives.

At present, ENICMA primarily concentrates on addressing two key areas: early identification and diagnosis of diseases in children, with a potential focus on developing alternative treatment methods. Additionally, ENICMA aims to prioritize research related to the environment and climate. As part of this long-term objective, ENICMA intends to establish an 'eco-informatics' sub-team dedicated to these endeavors.
